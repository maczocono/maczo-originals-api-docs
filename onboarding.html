The shared secret (important)

You generate the HMAC secret (e.g. openssl rand -hex 32) and send it to us out-of-band. maczo issues the operator_id + key_id; the secret value never travels on the wire and is not entered through any UI. It is used as its raw UTF-8 bytes in HMAC_SHA256(secret, canonical) — not hex/base64-decoded. maczo provisions your secret before your operator can go active; until then wallet calls fail closed with OPERATOR_CONFIG_ERROR. Keys can be rotated with an overlap window (X-Key-Id).

Currencies & settlement (important)

You settle in your own currency — every wallet callback is denominated in the currency you launch with, with no FX on the wire. During onboarding you tell us which currencies to enable (e.g. THB, or USDT) and your base currency; we confirm each one's authoritative minor-unit exponent. Supported: fiat 2 dp (USD EUR GBP THB SGD MYR PHP INR IDR BRL MXN AUD CNY HKD), fiat 0 dp (JPY KRW VND), crypto 8 dp (USDT USDC DAI BTC ETH BNB SOL TRX TON XRP DOGE LTC). Amounts are integer minor units at that exponent — always read currency_exp from each callback; parse *_minor as a 64-bit integer (exp-8 amounts can exceed 2^53). Per-currency bet limits are derived automatically (RTP-safe).

Steps

  1. Register. We onboard your operator and issue your operator_id + a signing key_id, and enable your currencies (base + the set you requested). You receive portal credentials.
  2. Implement the seamless wallet — the five endpoints in the Seamless Wallet API, verifying our HMAC-SHA256 header signature. Then call our Launch & Catalog API (/GameLogin, /GetGameList, /KickPlayer, /ReplayLogin) signed the same way — one signing routine for both directions.
  3. Configure your side in the portal: your wallet callback base URL + path prefix (default /seamless; the signed path is {prefix}/{func}) + your inbound IP allowlist.
  4. Test on the sandbox — point at https://originals-api-dev.maczo.co: /GameLogin → open the returned URL → place a bet → your wallet receives debit, then credit on a win (a loss never calls your wallet). Confirm idempotency (a replayed txn_ref returns duplicate:true, money moves once), insufficient-funds, and the read-only status(txn_ref). Confirm the debit/credit arrive in your launch currency with the expected currency_exp, and that amount_minor matches the stake placed. Your wallet is the source of the balance, so you fund the test player on your side (maczo never mints).
  5. Go live. We approve production; your operator flips to active and your callback host is enabled for production traffic.
  6. Reconcile. Use the portal reports (GGR / turnover / RTP-actual / settlement) against your own ledger; commission statements are produced per your agreed basis.

What you can configure in the portal

  • Integration: your wallet callback base URL + path prefix, inbound IP allowlist, signing keys.
  • Games: the subset of the catalogue you offer (GetGameList returns only your enabled games).
  • Currencies: the subset of supported currencies you accept (within what we enabled for you).
  • Limits: you may tighten per-game limits only — raise the min bet, lower the max bet / max win; you can never loosen the platform limits.
  • Commercial: your commission basis + rate (billing figure computed from aggregated totals).

What you can rely on

  • You are the source of truth for money — we never authorise a bet on a computed balance. Every money move is idempotent on a server-derived txn_ref (both sides dedupe).
  • Strict ordering — the stake is confirmed applied before any outcome is drawn or revealed; a losing round never calls your wallet.
  • Every outcome is provably fair and independently verifiable. Commission is a billing figure computed from aggregated totals — never deducted from a player's payout.
