Steps

  1. Register. We onboard your operator and issue your operator_id + a signing key_id. You receive portal credentials.
  2. Implement the seamless wallet — the five endpoints in the Integration wire contract §2, verifying our HMAC-SHA256 header signature (§3).
  3. Configure your side in the portal: your wallet callback base URL + your inbound IP allowlist.
  4. Test on the sandbox — point at https://originals-api-dev.maczo.co and run launch → bet → settle → reveal end-to-end. Confirm your wallet honours idempotency (txn_ref), insufficient-funds, and status.
  5. Go live. We approve production; your operator flips to active and your callback host is enabled for production traffic.
  6. Reconcile. Use the portal reports (GGR / turnover / RTP-actual / settlement) against your own ledger; commission statements are produced per your agreed basis.

What you can rely on

  • You are the source of truth for money — we never authorise a bet on a computed balance. Every money move is idempotent on a server-derived txn_ref (both sides dedupe).
  • Strict ordering — the stake is confirmed applied before any outcome is drawn or revealed; a losing round never calls your wallet.
  • Every outcome is provably fair and independently verifiable. Commission is a billing figure computed from aggregated totals — never deducted from a player's payout.
